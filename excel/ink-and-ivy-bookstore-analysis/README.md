# ☕ Bean & Bloom Café Sales Analysis

A mini Microsoft Excel project created to practice data cleaning, analysis, pivot tables, dashboard creation, and business reporting using a real-world coffee shop sales dataset.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes transaction data from a coffee shop to identify revenue trends, customer purchasing patterns, product performance, and store performance. The final deliverable is an interactive Excel dashboard supported by business insights.

---

- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning
- Excel Tables
- Formulas
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Dashboard Design
- Business Analysis
- Data Visualization

---

## Dashboard Features

- 💰 Total Revenue
- 🛒 Total Transactions
- ☕ Total Items Sold
- ⭐ Highest Revenue Category
- 📈 Revenue by Month
- 📊 Revenue by Product Category
- ⏰ Revenue by Hour
- 📅 Revenue by Weekday
- 🥇 Top 10 Products by Revenue
- 📍 Revenue by Store Location

---

## Key Insights

- Coffee generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
- Revenue increased steadily from January to June.
- Morning hours were the busiest sales period.
- Hell's Kitchen generated the highest revenue among the three stores.
- Barista Espresso was the top-performing product.
